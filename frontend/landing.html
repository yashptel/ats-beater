How It Works

Three steps to your
perfect resume

No templates. No guesswork. Just AI that understands what each employer is looking for.

01

Upload Your Resume

Upload your existing PDF resume. Our AI reads and structures every detail (experience, skills, education, projects) into a rich profile you can reuse.

02

Paste the Job Description

Paste the role, company, and job description. Our AI analyses the requirements and tailors your resume by rewriting bullet points, reordering sections, and highlighting relevant skills.

03

Download Your PDF

Get a professionally typeset PDF resume compiled with LaTeX, the same system used for academic papers and technical documents. Clean, consistent, ATS-friendly.

ATS Bot

Your AI resume agent,
built right in

Not just a generator. ATS Bot is an AI agent that reads, analyses, and edits your resume through natural conversation. Tell it what to change and watch it happen live.

Live Editing
Say "rewrite my first experience" or "add Kubernetes to skills" and the bot edits your resume in real time. Changes appear instantly, no page reload.
JD-Aware Analysis
The bot has full context of both your profile and the job description. It identifies gaps, suggests improvements, and reorders sections to match what the employer is looking for.
Tool-Powered Actions
Not just text responses. The bot uses tools to read your resume, apply structured edits, reorder projects, and trigger PDF recompiles automatically.
